Action / navigation bar sizing

The height of the action / navigation bar varies dependent upon the size and orientation of the screen you are designing for. Designs should be set on a roughly 48dp grid with 8dp gutters. Action bar design guidelines. Examples of recent Action Bars from popular apps..

48dp

Small Screen, Portrait

  • 48dp high
40dp

Small Screen, Landscape

  • 40dp high
56dp

Tablet Screen

  • 56dp high

When placing icons within the Action Bar you can either have them on display or hidden within the Menu Overflow.Google suggests that only actions which are F.I.T. (Frequent, Important or Typical) are displayed within and everything else is moved into the Menu Overflow. It's part of their remit to "Simplify productivity".
